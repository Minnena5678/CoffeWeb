How to make an Caffè Mocha

1. Prepare 1 shot of espresso and pour into a mug..

2. Froth your milk using your preferred method. For more information on how to froth your milk. 

3. Add mocha sauce into your mug and gently stir into the coffee.

4. Gently pour your frothed milk into your mug until it’s almost full.
